# MT Asphalt — Website Build Notes

## Target audience
Homeowners, business owners, and property managers in Orange County who need asphalt paving, driveways, parking lots, sealcoating, striping, and repair.

## Proposed site structure
1. **Home** — hero (phone CTA + free estimate), 22-years experience badge, verified active license badge, 4.9-star rating badge (215+ reviews), services overview, service-area line, testimonials slider.
2. **Services** — grid of services (from services.md). Each card links to a detail view.
3. **Service Area** — Orange County cities map/list.
4. **About** — Michael Tebb / owner-operated, 22 yrs, local roots, dedication to on-site quality.
5. **Gallery / Our Work** — photo grid showing professional paving and sealcoating.
6. **Contact / Free Estimate** — phone (714) 457-7111, contact form, physical address (Anaheim), service-area details.
7. **Testimonials** — Slider showcasing the premium 4.9/5 star ratings and curated customer reviews.

## Copy principles
- Lead with **longevity (22 yrs)** and **local Orange County** focus.
- Emphasize **free estimates** and **responsive, owner-involved service** ("Michael Tebb supervises every project").
- Prominently display **Verified CA License #836341** in headers and footers to establish immediate credibility.
- Mobile-first: click-to-call phone buttons must dominate the layout.

## Trust / SEO quick wins
- Display a prominent **Trust Seal** summarizing the **4.9 out of 5-star rating (215+ reviews)**.
- Local keywords: "asphalt paving Anaheim," "parking lot striping Orange County," "driveway resurfacing Westminster CA," etc.
- Add schema.org LocalBusiness markup (phone, address, areaServed, priceRange, founder, logo).
